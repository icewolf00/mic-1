A few weeks following the Surface unveil in New York ago a short video was posted showing students at Art Center College of Design in Pasadena wondering what Surface ‘blades’ could become. Blades is the codename for new accessories that can be connected to a Surface.  The Surface Remix Project is the first example of a blade and the students came up with many more at their 2 day DesignStorm.Check out the video above to see what they came up with.
