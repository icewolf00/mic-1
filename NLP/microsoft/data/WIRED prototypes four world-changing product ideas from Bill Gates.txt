WIRED invited Bill Gates to be the guest editor of their latest issue, and now they’ve brought four of his recent ideas for global good to life through a colorful set of prototypes by top design firms.One product, dubbed the Juice Box, is a portable device that can transform nearly any power source into a battery charge, bringing usable power to the billion-plus people who live off the grid globally.Hear more about the Juice Box from folks at Artefact, the firm that designed the prototype, in the video above, then head to the WIRED site to see all 4 prototypes.
