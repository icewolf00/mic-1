Got a Lumia? Nokia has a number of new apps out in their collection with Camera Extras being the pick of them. This neat little add on to the existing camera settings adds the following features  It’s been there for a month or so but I only just noticed it, along with other apps including Counters – a usage monitor for your call or data allowances, Play To for sharing photos, video and music to a DLNA compatible device, and Contact Share, that provides an easy way to share your contacts via SMS or email.Check them all by via the Nokia Collection on your device in the Marketplace app. Read more on Conversations by Nokia.Tweet
