Windows Phone has taken a radically different approach to the smartphone – putting people first and moving away from an app centric world. That’s not to say we don’t love apps, just that they can be more alive and more connected to each other (and your friends). That’s what sets Windows Phone apart for me – a bunch of people who bucked conventional wisdom and sat down and thought about how people really want to use their phones.…and Metro as a user interface.
