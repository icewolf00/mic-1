UFO sightings in the UK? No, not on this occasion. It’s part of a collection of 2700 Venue Maps from Bing that became available today. As Chris Pendleton notes on the Bing Maps Blog, our partnership with Nokia has helped Bing Maps to dramatically expand Venue Map coverage in the US and internationally.This update is primarily focused on shopping malls across North America, Europe, and Asia. For the UK, it’s just in time for the London Olympics. All you need to do is head bing.com/maps/venues and from there you can zoom in over your favorite mall to enter the experience.Here’s what Liverpool One, my home town mall, looks like.Tweet
