Steve Clayton - Editor - Microsoft News Center Staff

Microsoft Hardware is celebrating the Chinese New Year with a series of Limited Edition Year of the Dragon Mice. With a lot of Chinese friends and family, I can see I may have to place a bulk order here.Coming in traditional Chinese red or gold is the Wireless Mobile Mouse 3500 Year of the Dragon features an original dragon design created by premier artist, Nod Young – taking inspiration from the Nine-Dragon Wall of Beihai Park, a royal garden in China, and Dragon Ball – a popular cartoon If you’re not familiar with the 3500, it’s a notebook mouse with BlueTrack technology and an ambidextrous design, flanked with rubber side grips.The award winning Arc Touch Mouse is also getting in on the act with a limited edition version featuring art from Lin Xu Pan who incorporated the five-finger claw dragon – traditionally used for exclusive royal use and symbolizing nobleness. The red and gold Wireless Mobile Mouse 3500 Year of the Dragon is available now through online retailers.  The Arc Touch Mouse Year of the Dragon will be available from the Microsoft Store and other retailers soon.Tweet
