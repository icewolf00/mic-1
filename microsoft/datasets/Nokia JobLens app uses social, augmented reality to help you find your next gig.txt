Nokia has created a unique and quite useful new app for job seekers.JobLens sniffs out job leads from online sources (including Indeed, LinkedIn, Salary.com & more) as well as your physical surroundings via a nicely designed augmented reality viewer that displays opportunities nearby. It’s also a full-service tool for jobseekers – you can create and store your resume, apply to jobs, stay up to date on trends in your field and more.JobLens is available exclusively on Nokia Lumia devices running Windows Phone. You can download it now in the Windows Phone Store.
